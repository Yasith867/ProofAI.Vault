ProofAI Vault

====================================================
PROJECT SUMMARY
====================================================
ProofAI Vault is an onchain AI verification platform that transforms AI-generated outputs
(text, prompts, or datasets) into cryptographically verifiable proofs.

Each AI result is:
- Computed by AI
- Hashed for integrity
- Stored permanently (0G Storage ready)
- Committed onchain (0G Chain ready)
- Mintable as a Proof NFT

This project demonstrates FULL modular usage of:
- 0G Compute
- 0G Storage
- 0G Chain
- 0G DA
